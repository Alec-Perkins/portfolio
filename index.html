Projects

Spring-Mass System Simulation with MATLAB - Nov. 2023

I modeled an alternating sequence of springs and masses is connected to walls at both ends. The system is initially at rest in equilibrium and is then set in motion by displacing the masses arbitrarily. I used eigenvalue and eigenvector analysis to derive equations of motion, MATLAB to simulate the system dynamics, and LaTeX for the PDF report.

Microscope / Telescope Eyepiece Mount - Jun. 2023

I wanted a convenient way to take high-quality photos through any microscope or telescope eyepiece using the phone cameras we all carry in our pockets. Every combination of phone and eyepiece is different, so I designed an adjustable mount. It holds any phone and provides fine adjustment of both x- and y-axis, using small stepper motors to bring the camera into perfect alignment with the eyepiece.

Compressed Air Motor (CNC) - May 2023

This was a project for my machine shop class ME12s. We had to design parts for a compressed-air motor using SOLIDWORKS. We then had to build each part to required tolerances using CNC mills and lathes and assemble the motor. Mine was able to run at 3000 RPM, which exceeded the required 2000 RPM specifications.

Microphotography - Jan. 2023

I sampled some stagnant water from the UC Santa Barbara lagoon and saw a Bdelloid Rotifer (Rotaria Rotatoria) feeding on the algae that grows in the lagoon water. This one is about 300µm in length. I captured photos and video at 100x magnification.

Tensegrity Chair - Jun. 2022

I built and tested a special kind of chair called a Tensegrity Chair. These chairs appear precariously balanced and on the verge of collapse but are completely stable. This is an illusion much loved by Engineering Statics professors because they are like an interesting free-body diagram homework problem turned into furniture. I wanted to see for myself, so I built one. I then tested the load of the turnbuckle by incrementing the weight applied to the chair until the turnbuckle failed. These tests are documented in the report.
