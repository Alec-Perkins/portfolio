A little about me

My name is Alec Perkins. I’m a Mechanical Engineering major at UC Santa Barbara. I started coding in middle school, did front-end development, and later taught programming, but my personal projects usually combined art/design, software, and hardware. For that combination, Mechanical Engineering was ideal. As an undergraduate, I’m still building a broad base in fundamentals, but I am especially interested in Aerospace Engineering for space exploration and Biomedical Engineering for designing life-changing medical devices.
